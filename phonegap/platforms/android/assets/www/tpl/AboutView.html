Philosphy

Solar Wars is founded upon the idea of giving players a fun, friendly game that encourages your participation. We encourage your participation is seveal ways:

  • Free to Play, not Fee to pay. We absolutely do not approve of games that you have to pay to win. Solar Wars will never allow that
  • Open Source. The client and the server code will both be avaliable online at git hub eventually. Check back for the link.
  • We Listen. I, Jerome Hollon, will listen to your input. Find me on LINE with the username of Malfist. Tell me where you're from!
  • I Play Too. I'll play the game too, so I'll see it how you see it.

Privacy Policy

There isn't one. Assume we're keeping everything you put into this game, and we're selling it to your next door neighbor who secretly hates your guts, and doesn't like Fluffy either.

That being said, we're not going to sell your email address or whatever you used to login into the game with. We don't like it when other companies do that to us either.
